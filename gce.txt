Create a New Project on Google Cloud Platform (GCP):

Go to the Google Cloud Console (https://console.cloud.google.com/).

Click on the project dropdown menu at the top of the page and select "New Project".

Enter a project name, select your organization or billing account, and click "Create".

Enable App Engine:
In the Google Cloud Console, navigate to the "App Engine" section from the left-hand menu.
Click on "Get Started" if prompted to enable App Engine.
